Darrell "Dimebag" Abbott

Guitar virtuoso, Dimebag Darrell, founder of Pantera along with his brother Vinnie Paul Abbott, was a revolutionary guitar player and created an entire new genre of Groove Metal.

Guitars

Dimebag was famous for his use of Dean ML guitars like his heavily modified Dean From Hell one seen here. Dimebag originally won this guitar after winning a shredding competition, that he sold to purchase a yellow Pontiac Firebird. This same guitar started life as a maroon color, was modified with its classic lightning bolt design by another owner, and Dimebag eventually purchased back his original guitar that he had won.
